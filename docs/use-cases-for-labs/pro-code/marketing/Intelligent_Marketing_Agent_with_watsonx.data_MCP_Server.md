# Intelligent Marketing Agent with watsonx.data MCP Server

## Overview

Consider a retail business where a Marketing Manager (Sarah) needs to create personalized product recommendations and targeted email campaigns for customers. Typically, Sarah must manually analyze customer purchase history, product preferences, and transaction data across multiple systems, which takes at least 2-3 hours of effort per campaign. With the **Intelligent Marketing Agent**, she can efficiently generate personalized recommendations by asking something like "What should we recommend to customer John Smith?" and the agent will gather data from watsonx.data lakehouse, analyze purchase patterns, and generate targeted recommendations within minutes.

Additionally, she can generate complete email campaigns by asking "Generate an email campaign for customer C005" and the Intelligent Marketing Agent will:
- Retrieve customer profile and purchase history
- Identify preferred product categories
- Select top-rated products matching customer preferences
- Apply appropriate discount strategies
- Generate a structured, personalized email campaign

Sarah can also use the **Intelligent Marketing Agent** to get retail analytics insights. She can ask queries such as: "What are men purchasing more?", "Who are our top 10 customers by purchase count?", "Show me customers who haven't purchased in the last 3 months" and so on... the agent will retrieve relevant information from the watsonx.data lakehouse and provide actionable insights.

## Benefits

- **Personalized Recommendation Generation:** The Intelligent Marketing Agent can efficiently generate personalized product recommendations by analyzing customer data, purchase history, and product preferences from watsonx.data.
- **Automated Campaign Creation:** The agent can create complete email campaigns with personalized content, product recommendations, and discount strategies tailored to individual customers.
- **Retail Analytics & Insights:** The agent provides instant access to customer analytics, purchase patterns, and business intelligence through natural language queries against watsonx.data lakehouse.
- **Data-Driven Decision Making:** Leverages actual customer data stored in watsonx.data Iceberg tables to ensure recommendations are based on real purchase behavior and preferences.
